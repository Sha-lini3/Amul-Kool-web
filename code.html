Frequently Asked Questions

Does Amul Kool need to be refrigerated?

expand_more

While it can be stored at room temperature before opening, we highly recommend serving it chilled for the best refreshing experience. Once opened, it must be refrigerated and consumed quickly.

Is it suitable for lactose intolerant individuals?

expand_more

Amul Kool is a dairy-based product made with fresh milk. It is not suitable for individuals with lactose intolerance or dairy allergies.

What is the shelf life of Amul Kool?

expand_more

The typical shelf life is several months from the date of manufacture when stored properly in a cool, dry place. Please check the specific packaging for the exact expiration date.
